# 🗣️ Perfect Pronunciation

An AI-powered web app that helps learners master English pronunciation through real-time speech recognition and level-based practice.

🔗 **Live Demo:** [https://ntro077.github.io/perfect-pronunciation/](https://ntro077.github.io/perfect-pronunciation/)

---

## ✨ Features

- 🎯 **Level-based learning** – Choose from A1 (Beginner) to C2 (Mastery) – words and phrases adapt to your level
- 🎤 **Real-time speech recognition** – Speak into your mic and get instant pronunciation scores
- 🔊 **Calm voice guidance** – Click "Listen Guidance" to hear the correct pronunciation
- 📝 **Custom practice** – Add your own words or phrases and practice them with sample sentences
- 🎲 **Random practice mode** – Practice with words from your selected level

---

## 🛠️ How It Works

1. Select your **English level** (A1–C2)
2. Choose **Random Practice** or **Custom Practice** mode
3. Click **Speak & Check**, say the word clearly into your microphone
4. Get instant **pronunciation feedback and score**
5. Use **Listen Guidance** to hear the correct pronunciation
6. Click **Next Word** to practice another
