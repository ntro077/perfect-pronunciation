# 🗣️ Perfect Pronunciation

**An AI-powered web app that helps learners master English pronunciation through real-time speech recognition and level-based practice.**

🔗 **Live Demo:** [https://ntro077.github.io/perfect-pronunciation/](https://ntro077.github.io/perfect-pronunciation/)

---

## ✨ Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| 🎯 **Level-based learning** | Choose from A1 (Beginner) to C2 (Mastery) – words and phrases adapt to your skill level |
| 🎤 **Real-time speech recognition** | Speak into your microphone and get instant pronunciation scores |
| 🔊 **Calm voice guidance** | Click "Listen Guidance" to hear the correct pronunciation clearly |
| 📝 **Custom practice** | Add your own words or phrases and practice them with sample sentences |
| 🎲 **Random practice mode** | Practice with words from your selected English level |
| 📱 **Mobile friendly** | Works on smartphones, tablets, and desktop browsers |

---

## 🎯 English Levels Explained

| Level | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **A1** | Beginner – Basic words and simple phrases |
| **A2** | Elementary – Everyday words and common expressions |
| **B1** | Intermediate – Useful vocabulary for daily conversations |
| **B2** | Upper Intermediate – Academic and professional terms |
| **C1** | Advanced – Complex vocabulary and expressions |
| **C2** | Mastery – Sophisticated and nuanced language |

---

## 🛠️ How It Works

1. **Select your English level** (A1–C2) – words will match your skill level
2. **Choose a practice mode**:
   - **Random Practice** – Words from your selected level
   - **Custom Practice** – Your own personalized word list
3. **Click "Speak & Check"** and say the word clearly into your microphone
4. **Get instant feedback** with a pronunciation score (0-100%)
5. **Use "Listen Guidance"** to hear the correct pronunciation
6. **Click "Next Word"** to practice another word or phrase
